n-ordle

How to play

Guess n hidden five-letter words in n + 5 shared tries.

  • Every guess must be a valid five-letter word.
  • Each guess applies to every board at once.
  • The colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to each word.

Examples

WORDY

W is in the word and in the correct spot.

LIGHT

I is in the word but in the wrong spot.

ROGUE

U is not in the word in any spot.
