Highly Precise But Probably Inaccurate Bus Timings

Did you know LTA gives bus arrival times down to the second? Neither did I, until I poked around at LTA's DataMall API. They politely ask developers to round down to the nearest minute and slap "Arr" on anything under a minute away — but where's the fun in that?

So here it is, the full, unadulterated, LTA-provided second-by-second data: Highly Precise But Probably Inaccurate Bus Timings.
